About Me

I am an accomplished immunologist and translational scientist with over 20 years of experience advancing personalized cancer vaccines, neoantigen discovery, and cellular immunotherapies. My career has been dedicated to bridging discovery science with clinical application, with a focus on immuno-monitoring, T cell biology, and biomarker development in the context of cancer immunotherapy.

At top-tier institutions like City of Hope and MD Anderson Cancer Center, I have led immune-monitoring efforts for Phase I/II clinical trials, developed novel platforms for detecting rare antigen-specific T cells, and co-developed neoantigen identification pipelines validated with patient tumor samples. My work includes the successful development of DNA-based vaccines, TCR discovery systems using autologous B cells, and functional evaluation of CAR-T cell therapies.
